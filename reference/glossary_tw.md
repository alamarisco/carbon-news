## Notes

- Keep acronyms (CBAM, EU ETS, MRV, MSR, CSCF, DRI, EAF) in Latin on first mention with a
  Chinese gloss; thereafter the acronym alone is fine in body text.
- Titles (headline + LINE 中文標題) spell terms out in full Chinese — no acronyms.
- Body text stays plain (never bold); sub-headers bold.
